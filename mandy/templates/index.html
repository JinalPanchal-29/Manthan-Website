About Me

20 year old enthusiast cinematographer and a video editor. I am well versed with photography, illustrations and graphic work. I've worked for professional corporate clients as well as a freelancer for advertising agencies. Very familiar with Adobe Premiere Pro and Adobe Photoshop work.

I have a process to capture natural moments and authentic interviews in order to represent powerful perspectives through film. My goal is to create a long-lasting impact on each viewer through the power of human stories. I create impactful films through my cinematography and my editing.
